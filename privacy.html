Privacy Policy

Paste Tools is a Chrome extension designed to enhance the user's copy-paste workflow. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

What We Collect

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All actions are performed locally within your browser using Chrome APIs like contextMenus and scripting.

Permissions Used

  • contextMenus: To display custom right-click options.
  • scripting: To paste modified content into the input fields.
  • activeTab: To access the currently focused input field.
  • storage: To save your preset configurations locally.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.
